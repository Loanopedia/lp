Home Loan:Benefits

  • ✔️ Affordable Interest Rates – Competitive rates to make home buying easier.
  • ✔️ Flexible Repayment Tenure – Choose repayment tenure as per your convenience.
  • ✔️ Tax Benefits – Tax deductions under Section 80C & 24(b) of the Income Tax Act.
  • ✔️ Balance Transfer Facility – Transfer your existing loan to a lower interest rate.
  • ✔️ Top-up Loan Option – Get additional funds on your existing home loan.

Home Loan:Features

  • 🏠 Loan amount based on property value & income.
  • 📄 Quick processing & minimal documentation.
  • 🔄 Option for fixed or floating interest rates.
  • 💼 Available for salaried & self-employed individuals.
  • 📆 Pre-payment & foreclosure facility available.

Home Loan:Eligibility

  • ✔️ Age: 21-65 years (varies by lender).
  • ✔️ Employment Type: Salaried or Self-employed.
  • ✔️ Minimum Income: Depends on city & lender.
  • ✔️ Credit Score: Preferably 700+ for easy approval.
  • ✔️ Property Approval: The property should be legally clear.

Home Loan:Interest Rates & Charges

  • 💰 Interest Rate: 8% - 12% (varies by lender & applicant profile).
  • 📊 Processing Fee: 0.5% - 2% of loan amount.
  • ⚡ Prepayment Charges: Nil for floating-rate loans, charges may apply for fixed-rate.
  • 📉 Late Payment Penalty: Additional interest on delayed EMIs.

Home Loan:Documents Required

  • 🆔 KYC Documents – Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc.
  • 💰 Income Proof – Salary slips, ITR, bank statements.
  • 📜 Property Documents – Sale deed, agreement, NOC, etc.
  • 📝 Employment Proof – Offer letter, business registration (for self-employed).

Home Loan:Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)

  • ✔️ The property should be legally approved.
  • ✔️ The interest rate may be fixed or floating.
  • ✔️ Processing fee is non-refundable.
  • ✔️ Loan sanction depends on income & credit score.
  • ✔️ Prepayment charges may apply for fixed-rate loans.
