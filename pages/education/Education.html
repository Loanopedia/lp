Benefits

  • ✔️ Covers Tuition & Expenses – Includes fees, books, accommodation, and travel.
  • ✔️ Low-Interest Rates – Special rates for meritorious students.
  • ✔️ Flexible Repayment – Repayment starts after course completion.
  • ✔️ Tax Benefits – Deductions under Section 80E of the Income Tax Act.
  • ✔️ No Collateral for Small Loans – Loans up to ₹7.5 lakh without collateral.

Features

  • 🎓 Loan available for domestic & international education.
  • 📜 Covers undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional courses.
  • 💰 Moratorium period (repayment holiday) during the course & additional buffer time.
  • 🌎 Available for studies in top global universities.
  • 📆 Flexible tenure of up to 15 years.

Eligibility

  • ✔️ Applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • ✔️ Admission to a recognized institution is mandatory.
  • ✔️ Age: 18-35 years (varies by lender).
  • ✔️ Co-applicant (parent/guardian) may be required for certain loan amounts.
  • ✔️ Good academic record may help in securing better terms.

Interest Rates & Charges

  • 💰 Interest Rate: 6% - 12% (varies based on loan amount & university rank).
  • 📊 Processing Fee: 0% - 2% of the loan amount.
  • ⚡ Prepayment Charges: Nil in most cases.
  • 📉 Late Payment Penalty: Additional interest on delayed EMIs.

Documents Required

  • 🆔 KYC Documents – Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc.
  • 📜 Admission Letter – Proof of admission from the institution.
  • 💰 Income Proof – Parent/guardian salary slips, ITR, bank statements.
  • 📝 Fee Structure – Official document from the institution.
  • 🏠 Collateral Documents – If applicable, for high-value loans.

Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)

  • ✔️ Loan amount depends on institution, course, and income profile.
  • ✔️ Repayment starts after the moratorium period.
  • ✔️ Interest subsidy available under government schemes.
  • ✔️ Processing fee is non-refundable.
  • ✔️ Collateral/security may be required for higher loan amounts.
