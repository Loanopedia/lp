Construction Loan:Benefits

  • ✔️ Tailored for Building Homes – Funds for new construction or renovation.
  • ✔️ Flexible Disbursal – Amount released in phases as construction progresses.
  • ✔️ Competitive Interest Rates – Affordable rates based on borrower profile.
  • ✔️ Tax Benefits – Deductions under Section 80C & 24(b) of the Income Tax Act.
  • ✔️ Balance Transfer Facility – Transfer existing loan to a lower interest rate.

Construction Loan:Features

  • 🏗️ Loan covers land purchase & construction costs.
  • 📜 Disbursement in stages as per construction progress.
  • 💰 Option to convert into a regular home loan after construction.
  • 📝 Fixed or floating interest rate options available.
  • 📆 Pre-payment & foreclosure facility available.

Construction Loan:Eligibility

  • ✔️ Age: 21-65 years (varies by lender).
  • ✔️ Employment Type: Salaried or self-employed.
  • ✔️ Minimum Income: Depends on city & lender.
  • ✔️ Credit Score: Preferably 700+ for easy approval.
  • ✔️ Property Approval: Land & construction plan must be legally approved.

Construction Loan:Interest Rates & Charges

  • 💰 Interest Rate: 8% - 12% (varies by lender & applicant profile).
  • 📊 Processing Fee: 0.5% - 2% of loan amount.
  • ⚡ Prepayment Charges: Nil for floating-rate loans, charges may apply for fixed-rate.
  • 📉 Late Payment Penalty: Additional interest on delayed EMIs.

Construction Loan:Documents Required

  • 🆔 KYC Documents – Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc.
  • 📜 Income Proof – Salary slips, ITR, bank statements.
  • 🏠 Property Documents – Sale deed, approved construction plan, NOC, etc.
  • 📝 Contractor Agreement – If applicable, contract details with builder.

Construction Loan:Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)

  • ✔️ Loan disbursal happens in phases based on construction progress.
  • ✔️ Interest is charged only on the disbursed amount.
  • ✔️ The construction plan must be legally approved by local authorities.
  • ✔️ Processing fee is non-refundable.
  • ✔️ Loan sanction depends on income, credit score & project approval.
