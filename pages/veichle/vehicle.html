Vechicle Loan:Benefits

  • ✔️ Affordable Interest Rates – Competitive rates for vehicle purchases.
  • ✔️ Flexible Repayment Tenure – Options to suit your budget.
  • ✔️ Quick Approval Process – Fast processing for immediate needs.
  • ✔️ High Loan-to-Value Ratio – Get a significant portion of the vehicle's cost financed.
  • ✔️ Easy Documentation – Simplified paperwork for convenience.

Vehicle Loan:Features

  • 🚗 Loans available for new and used vehicles (cars, bikes, commercial vehicles).
  • 📄 Fixed and floating interest rate options.
  • 🔄 Options for balance transfer from existing loans.
  • 🛡️ Vehicle insurance bundled with loan options.
  • 📆 Prepayment and foreclosure facilities.

Vehicle Loan:Eligibility

  • ✔️ Age: 18-70 years (varies by lender).
  • ✔️ Employment Type: Salaried or Self-employed.
  • ✔️ Minimum Income: Determined by the lender based on loan amount.
  • ✔️ Credit Score: Preferably 700+ for better loan terms.
  • ✔️ Stable employment or business history.

Vehicle Loan:Interest Rates & Charges

  • 💰 Interest Rate: Varies (typically 7% - 15%) based on vehicle type and credit score.
  • 📊 Processing Fee: 0.5% - 2% of the loan amount.
  • ⚡ Prepayment Charges: May apply based on lender policies.
  • 📉 Late Payment Penalty: Additional interest or fees for delayed payments.

Vehicle Loan:Documents Required

  • 🆔 KYC Documents – Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc.
  • 💵 Income Proof – Salary slips, ITR, bank statements.
  • 📝 Vehicle Quotation/Invoice – Detailing the vehicle's cost.
  • 📜 Address Proof – Utility bills, rental agreements.
  • 💼 Employment/Business Proof – Offer letter, business registration.

Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)

  • ✔️ Loan amount depends on vehicle value and applicant's eligibility.
  • ✔️ Interest rate is determined by credit score and market conditions.
  • ✔️ Vehicle hypothecation is required for secured loans.
  • ✔️ Insurance is mandatory for the financed vehicle.
  • ✔️ Repayment schedule is fixed based on loan tenure.
