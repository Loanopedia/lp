Monthly Payment:

Benefits

  • ✔️ Instant Loan Disbursal – Quick approval for urgent medical expenses.
  • ✔️ No Collateral Required – Unsecured loan with minimal documentation.
  • ✔️ Flexible Repayment Options – Choose from EMIs or flexible tenures.
  • ✔️ Covers All Medical Expenses – Includes hospitalization, surgery, and treatment costs.
  • ✔️ Available for Pre & Post Treatment – Covers medical tests, medicines, and follow-up care.

Features

  • 🏥 Loan available for emergency & planned medical treatments.
  • 📜 Minimal paperwork required.
  • 💰 High loan amount up to ₹50 lakh (varies by lender).
  • 📆 Tenure ranging from 6 months to 5 years.
  • 🔄 Prepayment & foreclosure options available.

Eligibility

  • ✔️ Age: 21-65 years.
  • ✔️ Available for salaried & self-employed individuals.
  • ✔️ Minimum monthly income requirement (varies by lender).
  • ✔️ Credit Score: Preferably 700+ for easy approval.
  • ✔️ Co-applicant option available for higher loan amounts.

Interest Rates & Charges

  • 💰 Interest Rate: 9% - 18% (varies by lender & applicant profile).
  • 📊 Processing Fee: 0.5% - 2% of the loan amount.
  • ⚡ Prepayment Charges: Usually Nil after a few EMIs.
  • 📉 Late Payment Penalty: Additional interest on overdue EMIs.

Documents Required

  • 🆔 KYC Documents – Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport, etc.
  • 📜 Medical Bills & Treatment Estimate – Required for loan approval.
  • 💰 Income Proof – Salary slips, bank statements, or ITR.
  • 🏥 Doctor’s Prescription – Proof of medical necessity.

Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)

  • ✔️ Loan amount depends on income, credit score & medical requirement.
  • ✔️ Interest rate may be fixed or floating.
  • ✔️ Processing fee is non-refundable.
  • ✔️ Loan can be used for any medical expense, including surgery & treatment.
  • ✔️ Failure to repay may impact credit score and future loan eligibility.
