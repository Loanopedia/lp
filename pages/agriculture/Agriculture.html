Agricultural Loan:Benefits

  • ✔️ Low Interest Rates – Special rates for farmers & agricultural purposes.
  • ✔️ Flexible Repayment Tenure – Options based on crop cycles & earnings.
  • ✔️ Government Subsidies – Avail benefits under various agricultural schemes.
  • ✔️ No Collateral Required – Available for small and marginal farmers.
  • ✔️ Quick Processing – Faster approvals for urgent farming needs.

Agricultural Loan:Features

  • 🌾 Loan available for purchasing seeds, fertilizers, equipment, and irrigation.
  • 📜 Can be used for land purchase, improvement, or storage infrastructure.
  • 💰 Short-term & long-term financing options.
  • 🛡️ Crop insurance coverage available.
  • 📆 Repayment linked to harvesting cycles.

Agricultural Loan:Eligibility

  • ✔️ Must be a farmer, dairy owner, or involved in agricultural activities.
  • ✔️ Age: 18-70 years (varies by lender).
  • ✔️ Land ownership or lease agreement required.
  • ✔️ Credit history & past repayment behavior considered.
  • ✔️ Government-registered farmer groups/cooperatives are also eligible.

Agricultural Loan:Interest Rates & Charges

  • 💰 Interest Rate: 4% - 10% (subsidized rates may apply).
  • 📊 Processing Fee: 0% - 1.5% of the loan amount.
  • ⚡ Prepayment Charges: Usually nil for agricultural loans.
  • 📉 Late Payment Penalty: Additional interest or penalties based on lender policies.

Agricultural Loan:Documents Required

  • 🆔 KYC Documents – Aadhaar, Voter ID, PAN, etc.
  • 📜 Land Ownership/Lease Agreement Proof.
  • 💵 Income Proof – Bank statements, farm income details.
  • 📝 Agricultural Activity Proof – Crop patterns, business registration (if applicable).

Agricultural Loan:Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)

  • ✔️ Loan should be used strictly for agricultural purposes.
  • ✔️ Interest rate may vary based on loan amount and tenure.
  • ✔️ Subsidized interest available under government schemes.
  • ✔️ Loan approval is subject to creditworthiness and land ownership proof.
  • ✔️ Repayment tenure depends on crop cycles and farming income.
