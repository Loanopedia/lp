Personal Loan:Benefits

  • ✔️ No Collateral Required – Unsecured loan with no need for security.
  • ✔️ Quick Disbursal – Instant approval & fast fund transfer.
  • ✔️ Flexible Usage – Can be used for any personal expenses like travel, education, or medical needs.
  • ✔️ Easy Repayment – Choose tenure as per convenience (12 to 60 months).
  • ✔️ Minimal Documentation – Simple and hassle-free process.

Personal Loan:Features

  • 💰 Loan amount ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹50 lakh.
  • 📜 No need for guarantor or security.
  • 📆 Tenure ranging from 1 to 5 years.
  • 🔄 Fixed or floating interest rates available.
  • 📊 Prepayment & foreclosure options available.

Personal Loan:Eligibility

  • ✔️ Age: 21-60 years.
  • ✔️ Employment Type: Salaried or Self-employed.
  • ✔️ Minimum Monthly Income: ₹15,000 (varies by lender).
  • ✔️ Credit Score: Preferably 700+ for easy approval.
  • ✔️ Work Experience: Minimum 1 year for salaried, 2 years for self-employed.

Personal Loan:Interest Rates & Charges

  • 💰 Interest Rate: 10% - 24% (varies by lender & applicant profile).
  • 📊 Processing Fee: 1% - 3% of the loan amount.
  • ⚡ Prepayment Charges: Usually Nil after 6 months, varies by lender.
  • 📉 Late Payment Penalty: Additional interest on overdue EMIs.

Personal Loan:Documents Required

  • 🆔 KYC Documents – Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID.
  • 💰 Income Proof – Salary slips, bank statements, or ITR.
  • 📜 Employment Proof – Offer letter, business registration (for self-employed).
  • 🏦 Bank Statements – Last 3-6 months’ statements.

Personal Loan:Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)

  • ✔️ Loan approval is subject to income, credit score & repayment capacity.
  • ✔️ Interest rate may be fixed or floating.
  • ✔️ Processing fee is non-refundable.
  • ✔️ Loan tenure & amount depend on applicant’s profile.
  • ✔️ Non-repayment may impact credit score and future loan eligibility.
