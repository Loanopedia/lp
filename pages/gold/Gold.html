Gold Loan:Benefits

  • ✔️ Instant Loan Approval – Quick processing with minimal documentation.
  • ✔️ High Loan Amount – Get a loan up to 75% of gold's market value.
  • ✔️ No Income Proof Required – Loan based on gold value, not income.
  • ✔️ Flexible Repayment – Choose from bullet payment, EMI, or overdraft options.
  • ✔️ Secure Storage – Your gold is kept safe in bank lockers.

Gold Loan:Features

  • 🏦 Loan available against gold jewelry & coins.
  • 📜 No need for a credit score check.
  • 💰 Interest rates lower than personal loans.
  • 📆 Tenure ranges from a few months to 3 years.
  • 🔄 Option to renew the loan upon maturity.

Gold Loan:Eligibility

  • ✔️ Age: 18-75 years.
  • ✔️ Applicant must be the rightful owner of the gold.
  • ✔️ Gold should be of 18-24 carats purity.
  • ✔️ No need for salary proof or credit history.
  • ✔️ Available for individuals, business owners, and farmers.

Gold Loan:Interest Rates & Charges

  • 💰 Interest Rate: 7% - 15% (varies by lender & loan amount).
  • 📊 Processing Fee: 0.25% - 2% of the loan amount.
  • ⚡ Prepayment Charges: Usually Nil.
  • 📉 Late Payment Penalty: Additional interest on overdue amount.

Gold Loan:Documents Required

  • 🆔 KYC Documents – Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport, etc.
  • 📜 Address Proof – Utility bill, ration card, or bank statement.
  • 💰 No Income Proof Required.
  • 🏦 Gold Valuation Certificate – Provided by the lender.

Gold Loan:Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)

  • ✔️ Loan is sanctioned based on the weight and purity of gold.
  • ✔️ Interest rates vary based on the loan tenure and amount.
  • ✔️ Gold remains in lender’s custody until full repayment.
  • ✔️ Failure to repay may result in gold auction.
  • ✔️ Processing fee is non-refundable.
